The product

Three stages. One device that grows with you.

Stage 01 · Shipping
Cap the damage.
An 8-band multiband compressor with 3 ms lookahead listens through external MEMS microphones and hard-limits output at 85 dB SPL. Hybrid ANC cancels the dangerous floor. ISO 226 loudness compensation keeps the music shaped the way your ears expect. You still hear your friend. You still feel the drop. The damage stops.
Stage 02 · 2027
Read the room — and your brain.
Carbon-loaded silicone electrodes capture in-ear EEG. PPG, temperature, and IMU sensors map your physiological load. The device adapts to attention, fatigue, and cognitive state in real time. 94.2% cognitive load classification. Earlier than any smartwatch, by minutes.
Stage 03 · 2028+
An agent that lives in your ear.
Auditory Attention Decoding turns your focus into a command. The voice you want gets louder. The noise you don’t fades out. An ambient LLM coach lives at the edge — no phone, no screen, no tap. The first truly always-on interface that respects what you’re already doing.

Questions.

Is this earplugs?
No. Foam earplugs attenuate everything by blocking the ear canal. ARC listens, processes, and plays back a safe version of the same room in under 3 ms. You hear the space. You do not hear the damage.
Is this earbuds?
No. Earbuds replace the world with a stream. ARC’s default mode is the world — capped, shaped, and preserved — not a replacement for it.
Is this a hearing aid?
No. Hearing aids amplify what you are losing. ARC protects what you still have. Stage 02 adds biosensing; Stage 03 adds attention-based control. Neither is medical amplification.
Who is ARC for?
DJs, touring musicians, production crews, rave regulars, construction and industrial workers, parents of loud teenagers, and anyone whose life is louder than the average day and whose ears do not want to pay for it.
